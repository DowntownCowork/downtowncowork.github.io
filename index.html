    About us

    DownTown Co Work is a co-working office in the heart of Cape Town. Situated on the first floor of The Piazza on Church Square, this beautifully restored space is home to a collection of professional individuals and companies who value diversity, independence, privacy, hard work, beautiful aesthetics and easy city access.

    Features

    Reception desk 24hr CCTV and security
    24 hr finger print access control
    10 seater boardroom with telephone and video conferencing facilities
    "Fresh air" work space over looking Church Square
    High speed Internet (fibre and wifi)
    rint, scan and copy facilities
    Showers and lockers
    A honesty bar with drinks and snacks
    Deluxe coffee on tap
    Easy access to public transport, coffee shops, bars, restaurants, Church Square and The Company Gardens.
    Daily cleaning service
