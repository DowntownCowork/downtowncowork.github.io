    About us

    DownTown Co Work is a co-working office in the heart of Cape Town. Situated on the first floor of The Piazza on Church Square, this beautifully restored space is home to a collection of professional individuals and companies who value diversity, independence, privacy, hard work, beautiful aesthetics and easy city access.
